Drone Intruder Detection

What is Drone???

A drone, also known as an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV), is an aircraft that operates without a human pilot onboard. It can be controlled either autonomously by computers or remotely by a human operator on the ground1. Drones come in a variety of shapes and sizes, from small handheld devices to larger, more sophisticated aircraft1.

How It's Useful in Intruder Detection

1. Aerial Surveillance:

Drones offer an overhead perspective of the environment, enabling quick and effective coverage of vast areas. They can fly over restricted or sensitive areas to keep an eye out for any unauthorized entry or unusual activities. The live video streams from the droneâ€™s cameras enable security staff to promptly identify any trespassers.

2. Rapid Deployment:

In emergency situations or potential intrusions, drones can be swiftly put into action. Unlike security personnel on the ground, drones have the advantage of quickly reaching locations that are remote or difficult to access.
